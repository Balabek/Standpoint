About Me

At Standpoint Mastering, I strive to make your music sound the best it can. With over ten years of experience, I put definition and clarity at the center of my philosophy. I share your passion for your music, and I am here to make it epic.

Be it house, techno, trap, or other styles of electronic dance music, I will add value to your project. I use the best in analog and digital equipment to deliver world-class mixes and masters. My advisory and expertise will help your music sound big and convincing.

Services

Mixing

I can mix your record to emphasize its groove and to add the so much desired analog color and sheen. My goal is to comprehend the message of your song and to augment it by making it larger than life. Leaning on years of experience I strive to be your guide in helping you express your creativity.

Mastering

I can enhance the sound of your tracks and maximize their impact on various audio systems. By using analog and digital processing I shape your music to industry standards. My proprietary quality control method helps prepare your music for digital distribution.

Production

I can produce a track tailored to your specifications or I can help you as a co-producer of your own recordings. My streamlined process starts with a preliminary analysis to define your vision. Working in an iterative manner I help you realize your idea utilizing early feedback.
